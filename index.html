Services

What I Do

End-to-end mechanical design — from scanning and measuring existing parts to delivering print-ready models with tight tolerances.

01

3D CAD Modeling

Parametric solid modeling in Fusion 360. From concept sketches to fully constrained assemblies with drawings. Designed for manufacturability from day one.

02

Reverse Engineering

Recreating legacy or broken components from physical parts. Manual measurement, caliper data, and CAD reconstruction — resulting in a fully editable model.

03

Rapid Prototyping

Fast design–print–test iterations using FDM technology. Multiple material options, functional prototypes, and design validation — all in-house.

04

Custom Parts for 3D Printing

Print-optimized geometry — correct wall thickness, bridging, support strategies, and infill planning. Designed for FDM from the first sketch.
